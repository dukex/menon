# Privacy Policy for Menon

Last updated: 2024-09-20

## Introduction

Welcome to Menon, the app that transforms YouTube playlists into courses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

1. Email Address: Used to identify you and track your progress.
2. Course Progress: We record which lessons you've watched and which courses you've completed.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

1. Provide and improve our services
2. Track your progress through courses
3. Identify completed courses

## Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. Access your personal data
2. Correct inaccurate data
3. Request deletion of your data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Please review it periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@menon.courses.
